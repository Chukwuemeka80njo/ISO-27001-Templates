# 04_Planning

This folder contains all documentation related to ISMS planning activities required by ISO/IEC 27001:2022.  
Planning ensures the ISMS is properly structured, risk-based, and aligned with the organization's strategic objectives.

This section defines how risks are identified, evaluated, and treated, and how information security objectives are established and achieved.

---

## This section includes:

- ISMS risk assessment methodology  
- Identification of information security risks  
- Evaluation and scoring of risks  
- Risk treatment planning and decision-making  
- Establishment of ISMS objectives  
- Planning actions to achieve objectives  
- Determining resources needed for risk treatment  
- Integration of risk management into business processes  

---

## Included Templates

- Risk Assessment Methodology  
- Risk Assessment Template  
- Risk Register (ISO 27005 aligned)  
- Risk Treatment Plan  
- Information Security Objectives Template  
- Plan to Achieve ISMS Objectives  
- ISO 27001 Planning Worksheet
