# Audits

This folder contains all documentation, plans, and records related to the audit processes required by ISO/IEC 27001:2022.  
Audits help verify that the Information Security Management System (ISMS) is functioning effectively and meeting ISO 27001 requirements.

---

## Purpose of This Folder

Audit documentation provides evidence of:

- ISMS conformity with ISO/IEC 27001
- Effective operation of security controls
- Continual improvement and risk reduction
- Identification of nonconformities and corrective actions
- Internal audit program completeness and execution

These documents support both **internal audits** and **external certification audits**.

---

## Included Documents

Common templates and files found in this folder include:

- **Internal Audit Program** – Defines audit schedule, scope, and responsibilities
- **Internal Audit Plan** – Detailed plan for each audit cycle
- **Internal Audit Checklist** – Control-by-control verification based on Annex A
- **Internal Audit Report Template** – Documenting findings, evidence, and conclusions
- **Corrective Action Log** – Tracks remediation and follow-up activities
- **Nonconformity Register** – Records identified nonconformities and status
- **Audit Evidence Log** – Documents collected evidence during audit activities

---

## ISO/IEC 27001 References

- **Clause 9.2** — Internal Audit  
- **Clause 10.1** — Nonconformity and Corrective Action  
- **Annex A Controls** requiring monitoring, review, and evidence  

---

Maintaining this folder ensures your ISMS can be independently verified, audited, and certified.
