# 03_Leadership

This folder contains all documentation related to ISMS leadership responsibilities, top management commitment, and governance requirements defined in ISO/IEC 27001:2022.

Leadership involvement ensures the ISMS is aligned with business goals, receives the necessary resources, and is effectively communicated across the organization.

---

## This section defines:

- Leadership roles and responsibilities for the ISMS  
- The Information Security Policy  
- Organizational roles for information security  
- Communication of the importance of information security  
- Strategic direction for ISMS implementation  
- Integration of ISMS requirements into business processes  
- Demonstrating leadership and commitment  

---

## Included Templates

- ISMS Leadership Responsibilities Matrix  
- Information Security Policy  
- ISMS Governance Charter  
- Roles, Responsibilities & Authorities Document  
- Top Management Commitment Statement  
- ISMS Communication Plan
