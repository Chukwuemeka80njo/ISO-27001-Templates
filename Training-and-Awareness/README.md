# Training and Awareness

This folder contains all documentation, schedules, records, and templates related to Information Security training and awareness activities required under ISO/IEC 27001:2022.

Training and awareness ensure all personnel understand their security responsibilities and support the effectiveness of the ISMS.

---

## Included Templates and Documents

- **Annual Training Plan** – Overview of planned ISMS training activities  
- **Security Awareness Presentation** – Templates for staff training sessions  
- **Training Attendance Log** – Record of participants and completion status  
- **Role-Based Security Training Materials** – Tailored content for specific job functions  
- **Phishing Simulation Results Template** – Evidence of awareness program effectiveness  
- **Training Evaluation Form** – Measures comprehension and training impact  

---

## Purpose

Training and awareness documentation helps ensure:

- Personnel understand their information security obligations  
- Management can demonstrate compliance with ISO 27001 requirements  
- The ISMS remains effective through informed and engaged staff  
- Role-based responsibilities are clearly communicated  

---

## ISO/IEC 27001 References

- **Clause 7.2** – Competence  
- **Clause 7.3** – Awareness  
- **Annex A: A.6.3** – Information Security Awareness, Education, and Training
