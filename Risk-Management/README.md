# Risk-Management

This folder contains all documentation, tools, and templates required to perform risk assessment and risk treatment according to ISO/IEC 27001:2022 and ISO/IEC 27005.

Risk management is a core component of the ISMS and supports informed decision-making, prioritization, and control selection.

---

## This section includes:

- Risk assessment methodology  
- Identification of risks, threats, vulnerabilities, and impacts  
- Risk evaluation and scoring  
- Determination of inherent and residual risk  
- Risk treatment planning and control selection  
- Linking risks to Annex A controls  
- Statement of Applicability (SoA)  
- Approval, monitoring, and review of risks  

---

## Included Templates

- Risk Assessment Methodology (ISO 27005-aligned)  
- Risk Assessment Template  
- Risk Register (Full ISO format)  
- Risk Treatment Plan (RTP)  
- Risk Acceptance Form  
- Statement of Applicability (SoA) Template  
- Threat & Vulnerability Catalog  
- Asset Inventory Template  
- Risk Review & Monitoring Log  

---

## ISO/IEC 27001:2022 References

- **Clause 6.1.2** – Information security risk assessment  
- **Clause 6.1.3** – Information security risk treatment  
- **Clause 8.2 / 8.3** – Operational risk assessment & treatment execution  
- **Annex A** – Control mapping for risk treatment  
- **ISO/IEC 27005** – Risk management framework
