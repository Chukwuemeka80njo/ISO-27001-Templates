# 02_Context-of-the-Organization

This folder contains all documentation related to understanding the internal and external factors that influence the Information Security Management System (ISMS), as required by ISO/IEC 27001:2022.

Understanding the organization’s context ensures the ISMS is aligned with business objectives, stakeholder needs, and operational realities.

---

## This section defines:

- Internal issues (e.g., processes, technologies, culture)
- External issues (e.g., legal, regulatory, market forces, suppliers)
- Relevant stakeholders (interested parties)
- Their expectations related to information security
- ISMS boundaries influenced by contextual factors
- Dependencies and interfaces affecting the ISMS

---

## Included Templates

- Context of the Organization Worksheet  
- Internal & External Issues Register  
- Interested Parties Register  
- Needs & Expectations of Interested Parties  
- ISMS Requirements Mapping Document  
- Dependencies & Interfaces Register
