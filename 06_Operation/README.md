# 06_Operation

This folder contains all documentation related to the operational activities required to run and maintain the Information Security Management System (ISMS) according to ISO/IEC 27001:2022.

Operational processes ensure that information security controls are implemented, monitored, and managed effectively as part of day-to-day business operations.

---

## This section includes:

- Operational planning and control  
- Implementation and management of Annex A controls  
- Change management  
- Supplier and third-party security management  
- Business continuity and disaster recovery operationalization  
- Backup and restoration procedures  
- Secure system operation activities  
- Capacity and performance management  
- Technical and administrative control execution  

---

## Included Templates

- Operational Planning & Control Procedure  
- Supplier Management Procedure  
- Change Management Procedure  
- Secure Operations Checklist  
- Backup & Restore Procedure  
- Capacity & Performance Monitoring Log  
- Operational Risk Log  
- Day-to-Day ISMS Activity Worksheet

## ISO/IEC 27001:2022 References

- **Clause 8.2** – Information security risk assessment (operational execution)  
- **Clause 8.3** – Information security risk treatment (implementation of controls)
