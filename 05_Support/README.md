# 05_Support

This folder contains all documentation related to organizational support functions required to establish, implement, maintain, and continually improve the ISMS under ISO/IEC 27001:2022.

Support processes ensure the ISMS has the necessary resources, competencies, communication channels, and documentation controls to operate effectively.

---

## This section includes:

- Resource allocation for ISMS operations  
- Personnel competence and training requirements  
- Awareness programs and communication processes  
- Documented information management  
- Control of records and version management  
- Tools and resources supporting the ISMS  

---

## Included Templates

- ISMS Resource & Roles Matrix  
- Competence & Training Requirements  
- Training Needs Analysis Form  
- ISMS Communication Plan  
- Document Control Procedure  
- Documented Information Register  
- Record Retention & Disposal Schedule  
- Template for Managing Controlled Documents  

---
